Welcome to Surgeries Online

An automated software for local surgeries

The Surgeries Online is a project which focuses on health care delivery aiming to ease, enhance and further automate interactions between the major actors especially focusing on the patients. Surgeries and hospitals can register with the system while various user types are associated with a surgery. The system then provides functionalities which support various actions an interactions between users associated with a given surgery e.g. booking/managing of appointments with nurses and doctors, making and management of prescriptions etc

Improved services for patients

We aim to improve services for patients by enabling direct access to services provided by their surgeries. This includes booking and managing of appointments with doctors and nurses, accessing and onlinle payment for prescriptions, managing of referrals etc. Patients also has access to their history (appointments, prescriptions and referrals over time)

We also support patient transfer form one surgery to another for patients that may change surgery for any personal reasons. Patients can also print a report containing such histories

Imroved services by surgeries

We aim to improve on the services offered by surgeries to their patients. By enabling easier access to services by and improving the interaction between patients and hospital staff.
